Our Program is Right For You

TESOL 120

Most countries, and all accredited private U.S. schools, require 120 hours of TESOL instruction to qualify for an international work visa or for a job. Our complete 120 hours certification program will cover everything you will need to know to go in front of an ESL/EFL class with confidence and professionalism.

  • Trends in Language teaching
  • How to teach the students of today
  • Intercultural communication
  • Second language teaching techniques
  • Classroom management
  • Teaching different ages
  • Teaching listening, speaking, reading, writing, and grammar
  • Testing and assessing methods
  • Curriculum design

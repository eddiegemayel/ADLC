Real Life Experience Experiences

Real life experiences is a program of " blended learning" 12 months designed to help students acquire the English language using an approach that facilitates cognitive processing. Each lesson is multi - level , which allows us to meet the needs of every student , regardless of their level of knowledge.

  • INTERACTIVE GAMIFICATION

    Enter the state of flow: lose sense of time, ignore surrounding distractions and focus on learning

  • ADAPTIVE LEARNING

    Although some individual students may take longer than others to successfully complete the games, the end result is always the same - 100% mastery. Every student is given an equal opportunity to come to class fully prepared.

  • SPACED REPETITION

    Experts state that approximatley 40% of what we learn is lost by the end of the day. Through spaced repitition games, our students can improve memory retention rates by up to 90%.
